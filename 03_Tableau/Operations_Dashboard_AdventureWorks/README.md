# 📊 Operations Dashboard – Sales & Inventory Trends

This project was completed as part of the **Turing College Data Analytics Program** using **Tableau**.

## Overview

The objective was to design an interactive dashboard that helps business stakeholders monitor **sales performance** and **inventory management** within the **USA market**. The dashboard enables users to compare online and offline sales, monitor inventory trends, and identify high-performing products.

## Business Questions

- How does online sales performance compare to offline sales?
- How do inventory levels change over time, and what is their impact on sales?
- Which products perform best across different sales channels?

## Key KPIs

- Total Sales by Channel (Online vs. Offline)
- Order Volume
- Stock Turnover Rate
- Top-Selling Products by Sales Channel
- Inventory Trends
