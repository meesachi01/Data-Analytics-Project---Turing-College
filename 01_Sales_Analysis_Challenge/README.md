# 📊 Sales Analysis Challenge

## Overview

This project was completed as the first graded assignment during the **Data Analytics Program at Turing College**.

The objective was to analyze sales data from the **AdventureWorks** dataset using **Google Sheets** and provide actionable business insights through an interactive dashboard and structured analytical approach.

---

## Business Problem

The business wanted to better understand its sales performance and customer behavior by answering the following questions:

- How do monthly sales change over time?
- What is the difference between online and offline sales?
- How has delivery performance changed over time?
- Who are the top-performing salespeople?
- Which countries generate the most revenue?
- What are the most common reasons for sales?
- What additional insights can support better business decisions?

---

## Dataset

The analysis was based on the following AdventureWorks tables:

- Sales Order Header
- Sales Reasons
- Employee
- Sales Territory

The dataset was provided in Google Sheets with selected columns to improve performance.

---

## Tools Used

- Google Sheets
- Pivot Tables
- Charts & Dashboards
- Data Cleaning
- Data Visualization

---

## Project Workflow

The analysis followed the six-step Data Analytics process:

1. **Ask** – Defined the business questions and success metrics.
2. **Prepare** – Collected and reviewed the required datasets.
3. **Process** – Cleaned, validated, and organized the data.
4. **Analyze** – Explored trends, KPIs, and performance metrics.
5. **Share** – Created dashboards and visualizations to communicate findings.
6. **Act** – Provided business recommendations based on the analysis.

---

## Dashboard Highlights

The final dashboard included:

- 📈 Monthly Sales Trend
- 🌐 Online vs Offline Sales Comparison
- 🚚 Delivery Performance (Order Date vs Ship Date)
- 🏆 Top Salespeople by Revenue and Order Volume
- 🌍 Top Performing Countries
- 💡 Top Sales Reasons
- 📊 Additional business insights and recommendations
