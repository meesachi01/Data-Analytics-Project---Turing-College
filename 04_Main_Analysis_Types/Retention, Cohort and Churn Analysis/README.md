# 📊 Subscription Cohort Analysis

This project was completed as part of the **Turing College Data Analytics Program** using **Google BigQuery** and **Tableau**.

## Overview

The objective was to analyze **weekly customer retention** by grouping users into subscription cohorts and tracking their activity over the first **6 weeks** of their subscription lifecycle. The analysis helps identify retention patterns, customer churn, and the performance of different acquisition cohorts.

## Business Questions

- How does customer retention change over the first six weeks after subscribing?
- Which weekly cohorts demonstrate the highest retention?
- At what point do customers churn most frequently?
- How do retention trends vary across different cohorts?
