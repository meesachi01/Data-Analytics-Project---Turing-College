# 🧪 A/B Testing Analysis – Fast Food Marketing Campaign

## Overview

This project analyzes the effectiveness of three marketing campaigns conducted by a fast food chain using **A/B testing** and statistical analysis. The objective was to determine whether differences in campaign performance were statistically significant and to identify the most effective promotional strategy.

## Business Value

A/B testing enables businesses to make evidence-based decisions by validating whether changes in marketing, products, or user experience lead to measurable improvements. It helps reduce uncertainty and optimize business performance.

## Skills Demonstrated

- Statistical Analysis
- A/B Testing
- Hypothesis Testing
- Marketing Analytics
- Data Interpretation
- Business Insights
- Data Visualization
