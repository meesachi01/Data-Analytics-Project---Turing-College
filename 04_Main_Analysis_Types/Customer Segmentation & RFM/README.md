# 👥 Customer Segmentation & RFM Analysis

## Overview

Customer segmentation helps businesses better understand their customers by grouping them based on purchasing behavior. One of the most widely used techniques is **RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary) Analysis**, which identifies customer value based on how recently they purchased, how often they buy, and how much they spend.

In this project, I performed an RFM analysis to segment customers into meaningful groups, enabling targeted marketing strategies, improved customer retention, and more effective business decision-making.

## Business Value

This analysis helps businesses to:

- Identify high-value and loyal customers
- Detect customers at risk of churning
- Improve customer retention strategies
- Personalize marketing campaigns
- Increase customer lifetime value (CLV)
- Optimize marketing spend
