# 💰 Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) Analysis

## Overview

Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) analysis estimates the total revenue a customer is expected to generate throughout their relationship with a business. Understanding CLV enables organizations to identify high-value customer segments, optimize customer acquisition costs, and make informed decisions on retention and marketing investments.

In this project, I analyzed customer cohorts to estimate Customer Lifetime Value and evaluate how customer value evolves over time.

## Business Value

This analysis helps businesses to:

- Estimate long-term customer value
- Identify high-value customer cohorts
- Measure revenue generated over the customer lifecycle
- Optimize customer acquisition and retention strategies
- Support data-driven marketing and budgeting decisions
